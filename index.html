Why families choose us

A strong foundation for lifelong learning.

New Foundation Academy keeps school life clear, structured and child-centred. Pupils learn in a calm environment where teachers know their strengths, support their gaps and expect steady progress.

The school experience is built around sound academics, good manners, practical skills, spiritual and moral formation, and regular partnership with parents.

Admissions

Enrolment made direct and personal

Families can begin with a visit, a conversation with the school team and a simple review of the child's current learning level.

  1. Visit the school Meet our team and see the learning environment.
  2. Complete registration Submit basic pupil and parent information.
  3. Assessment and placement We place each learner where they can grow with confidence.
